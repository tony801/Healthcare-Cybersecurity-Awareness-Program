Our Mission

At Cybersafe, we're all about making sure that healthcare stays safe from online threats. We teach doctors, patients, and others how to keep medical info secure and help healthcare systems stay strong against cyber attacks. With our easy-to-follow online lessons and programs, we want to build a culture where everyone in healthcare is alert, works together, and keeps learning about cybersecurity. Together, we're focused on keeping patient info private, keeping trust in healthcare strong, and making sure everyone stays safe and protected.
